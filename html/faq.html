Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our EV charging services

You can find the nearest charging station using our mobile app or website. We have stations strategically located throughout Jaipur for your convenience.

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, and our mobile app wallet. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Charging time depends on your vehicle's battery capacity and the charging station's power output. Typically, a full charge takes between 30 minutes to 2 hours.

While reservations are not mandatory, we recommend booking a slot through our app to ensure availability, especially during peak hours.

Our stations are monitored 24/7. If you encounter any issues, please report them through our app or contact our support team immediately.
